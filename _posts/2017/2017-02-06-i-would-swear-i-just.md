        <p>Don&#39;t forget you mark exp when you hit other people&#39;s flag. Not when you&#39;re flags get hit.<br />The GM should ask each player in turn at the end of the session who they think triggered their flags. It&#39;s that person that marks exp.<br /><br />Some GM allow you to gain 1 exp per session this way no matter how many flags you triggered.  Others give 1exp per flag triggered. I would limit it to 1exp per player. So in a party of 4 you could mark up to 3 exp by hiting every other characters flags at least once.</p>
