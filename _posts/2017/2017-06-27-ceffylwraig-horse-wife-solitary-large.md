<b>Ceffylwraig</b> <b>(Horse Wife)</b><br /><i>solitary, large, cautious, organized, magical, spirit</i><br /><b>HP</b> 20, <b>Armor</b> 0<br />Kicking hoofs: [w]2d8+3 (<i>hand, close, forceful</i>)<br />   • Manifest as a majestic, beautiful horse<br />   • Slip free of any restraint<br />   • Guide the herd like it was a single living entity<br />   • Curse someone with wild, unbridled emotions<br /><b>Instinct</b>: to be fiercely independent<br /><br />A herd of wild horses has a life, nay, a spirit all its own, a spirit as free and fickle as the wind. Take care, you who would poach wild horses for your own purpose, lest you find your heart turned against you!<br /><br /><i>Something interesting</i>: A ceffylwraig sees herself more as queen than wife: proud and haughty, disdainful of the weak and the slow, the herd her subjects more than her children<br /><br /><i>Something useful</i>: a ceffylwraig is scornful and suspicious of praise or fancy words, but can be appeased by thoughtful gifts, offered with appropriate respect from proud women of noble bearing<br />   ○ <i>What sort of gift do the ceffylwraig most value?</i><br />   ○ <i>What sort of gift will do in a pinch?</i><br /><br /><br /><a rel="nofollow" class="ot-hashtag" href="https://plus.google.com/s/%23stonetop/posts">#stonetop</a>
