A humble holiday gift for Tavern patrons...<br /><br />Ravenloft is one of those classic D&amp;D modules like Barrier Peaks or The Lost City that gets a lot of deserved love and has some great ideas that are easy to harvest for Dungeon World. A variety of Ravenloft  inspired &quot;labyrinth move&quot; adaptions are floating around, several very good. I couldn&#39;t find the one I wanted/needed/remembered or imagined awhile back so I was forced to make one of my own. Hopefully it will help with any vampire hunts you have planned...I call my version Vampire Castle. Feedback appreciated!<br /><br />Happy Holidays! Avoid Vampires!
