Another poll for my spell compendium! I&#39;m writing the Wish spell and need to know what everyone thinks. Should it have a list of effects it can produce, or should it be open to any wish? The spell mentions how dangerous it is, since the outcomes can be disastrous. I feel like that would be more true if there were no limitations to what someone wishes for. Let me know what you guys think!<br />
