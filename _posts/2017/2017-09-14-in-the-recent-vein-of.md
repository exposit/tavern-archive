In the recent vein of Discern Realities dungeon starters, I humbly present to you &quot;The Curse of Drazhu&quot;. This is a rough draft that I whipped up from a combination of the original Discern Realities starter, Jeremy Strandberg&#39;s excellent elaboration and &quot;The Curse of Strahd&quot; conversion. I recently introduced a big group of friends to DW with Jason Morningstar&#39;s &quot;Slave Pit of Drazhu&quot; game demo. They dug it so I thought I&#39;d spin it into a larger tale. <br /><br />Please have a look and comment on the doc or below in the comments with your feedback. I&#39;d especially love some suggestions for more unique choices as some of them currently are ripped straight from the excellent work that came before. Thanks and I hope you like it.
