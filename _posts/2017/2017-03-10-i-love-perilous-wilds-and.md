I LOVE Perilous Wilds (and have ordered my print copy) but I hate rolling so many dice when I just want some quick dungeon ideas, so I built a one-roll dungeon generator, which borrows heavily from PW and a couple other sources. Roll 1 of each die together (d4 through d20) and consult the tables (see link).<br /><br />I&#39;ve used this several times for my own games (and will be creating one for regions, cities, factions, and possibly NPC&#39;s) and decided to put it into a google doc to get some feedback. I&#39;m not entirely happy with the d20 table, so any thoughts or ideas on any of it would be appreciated.<br /><br /><a href="https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1MwzY449bz5Xk6NwssT4alFgxxKtVQO9JNWt31dqBEMw/edit?usp=sharing" class="ot-anchor">https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1MwzY449bz5Xk6NwssT4alFgxxKtVQO9JNWt31dqBEMw/edit?usp=sharing</a>
