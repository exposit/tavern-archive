I&#39;m going to playtest a variation of the <span class="proflinkWrapper"><span class="proflinkPrefix">+</span><a class="proflink" href="https://plus.google.com/108530078404383929502" oid="108530078404383929502">Jason Cordova</a></span> labrynth move to simulate something I can the &quot;Death Star Hustle.&quot; Remember when Han and Luke and company were running around in the Death Star, dodging Stormtrooper squads, kind of a combination of a chase scene with exploration and running fights? The idea is to run a scene like that in Dungeon World. I can think of lots of situations where the party is inside an enemy base or deep in a dungeon where &quot;all sectors have been alerted to you presence!&quot;<br /><br />Basically I&#39;m thinking if they get a 10+ on the roll, they get a hold and don&#39;t encounter guards.<br /><br />On a 7-9, they get a hold but have to fight or evade guards.<br /><br />On a 6- they have to backtrack (lose a hold).<br /><br />Once they have 5 hold they can find the Oblivion Altar (or hanger bay, or prison level).<br /><br />What do you guys think? I&#39;ll report back once my players run through it...
