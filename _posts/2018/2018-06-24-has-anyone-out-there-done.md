Has anyone out there done any work statting up hazards or traps as if they were monsters?<br /><br />For example:<br /><br /><b>Flash Flood</b><br /><i>natural</i>, <i>terrain</i><br /><br />Instinct: to flow<br />● Soak someone or something <br />● Drag something or someone away<br />● Block a path<br /><br />or <br /><br /><b>Crude Spiked Pit Trap</b><br /><i>constructed</i>, <i>concealed</i>, <i>static</i><br />Sharpened stakes: 2d6 damage (<i>messy</i>)<br /><br />Instinct: to lie in wait<br />● Give out underfoot<br />● Impale someone<br />● Betray signs of its presence<br /><br /><br />I&#39;m not really sure it&#39;s worth it, but I&#39;d love to see other, similar stuff that&#39;s been done before.
