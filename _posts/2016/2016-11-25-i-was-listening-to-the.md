        <p>It&#39;s an inverse bond. One character has a flag (like &quot;gullible&quot;) which effectively creates a move for everyone else in the group like &quot;if you lie to me and I believe it, we both mark XP&quot;.<br /><br />It&#39;s especially useful for games where attendance is variable,  since bonds to missing players tends to get dull.</p>
