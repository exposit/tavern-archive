Interested?<br /><br />We&#39;re looking for groups who:<br />•	Play every 2-3 weeks (or more!)<br />•	Can commit to actually playing 3+ sessions<br />•	Are willing to specifically try out certain content<br />•	Will ask questions &amp; provide thoughtful feedback<br />•	Can put up with potentially frequent changes <br /><br />If that sounds like you, please speak up in the comments.<br /><br />If you&#39;re interested in playtesting, but don&#39;t have a group that meets the requirements above, let us know and we&#39;ll see if we can&#39;t put some of y&#39;all together. <b>EDIT</b>: If you do, please post your time zone (e.g. GMT -5) so we help identify possible Hangout groups.<br /><br />If you&#39;d like to take a look but aren&#39;t really interested in playtesting, that&#39;s cool, too!  Click the  <a rel="nofollow" class="ot-hashtag" href="https://plus.google.com/s/%23Stonetop/posts">#Stonetop</a>   hashtag and check out the content. It might not be totally up-to-date but it&#39;ll give you an idea of what we&#39;re working on.  ﻿
